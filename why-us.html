How We Operate

The Levant
Standard.

These aren't aspirational values on a wall. They're operational rules that govern every project we take on. If we violate any of them, we owe you work at no charge.

01
Scope First. Always.
We write down exactly what we'll build, what it costs, and when it's done — before we start a single pixel. No vague estimates. No "we'll figure it out as we go."
If you can't hold us accountable to a written deliverable, we won't take the project. This protects you and forces us to think clearly before we commit.
02
Ownership Is Defined Up Front.
Your proposal spells out what is transferred, what stays third-party or licensed, and what access you receive at launch. We do not leave ownership vague or use access as leverage.
Most agencies keep ownership terms fuzzy until the project is already underway. We put those terms in writing before work starts so you know what you are paying for and what happens at handoff.
03
SEO Is Not Optional.
Every website we build is structured for search from day one. Not as a bolt-on, not as an upgrade — as a non-negotiable part of the build process.
A beautiful website that Google can't find is a brochure that nobody reads. Technical SEO is built into our process the same way mobile responsiveness is — you don't pay extra for it.
04
We Tell You What We Can't Do.
If your goals are outside what we can honestly deliver, we'll say so upfront. We'd rather lose a project than oversell and underdeliver.
This sounds obvious, but almost no agency actually does it. We've turned down projects because the client's expectations couldn't be met in the budget they had. We'll do the same for you.
05
No Ghosting. Ever.
You get a response within 24 hours. You see progress updates throughout the project. You are never left wondering what's happening with work you already paid for.
This shouldn't be a differentiator. It is. Too many Metro Detroit businesses have been burned by agencies that go dark mid-project. We have a policy: if we ghost you for more than 48 hours without warning, we refund the day rate.
