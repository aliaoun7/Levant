What does "SEO-ready" actually mean?
+

Proper headings, metadata, page structure, internal linking, and indexing readiness. It's the technical foundation — not a full ongoing SEO campaign. But it's what most businesses are missing.

Can you redesign my existing site?
+

Absolutely. We keep what's working and improve structure, visuals, speed, and mobile experience. You don't need to start from zero — we can work with what you have.

How long does a website take?
+

Most projects are completed in 1-2 weeks depending on scope. We give you a clear timeline before work begins so there are never any surprises on delivery date.

Do I own everything when it's done?
+

Yes. All source files, code, brand assets, and account access are transferred to you on delivery. Nothing is held back or kept hostage behind a subscription.
